Chicken Recipe

Description

A meal that even you could make. I mean come on, its chicken in a pan with some vegetables around it. How hard could it be? Just make sure that you fully cook the chicken and really no one can complain. It could taste like the bottom of your shoe and no one would complain so long as it doesn't give them salmonella. Give them salmonella, however, and they may want to have a word with the chef. Oh also, potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Potatoes
  • The Will to Live

Steps

  1. Season your chicken
  2. Place potatoes in pan first, follwed by the chicken
  3. Place in 375 degree F oven
  4. Stare into the abyss and hope nothing stares back
  5. Remove, rest, and enjoy!
