Steak Recipe

Description

This dish is certain to please on date night. Using high heat to get a great sear, as well as implimenting aromatic herbs, this dish is sure to impress. Rosemary and thyme are very tasty, and really balance the heartiness of the dish.

Ingredients

  • T-Bone Cut Steak
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the pan
  2. Sear both sides of steak
  3. Add aromatics
  4. Baste butter over aromatics
  5. Remove, rest, enjoy!
