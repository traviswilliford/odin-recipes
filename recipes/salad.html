Salad Recipe

Description

This dish is sure to make people think that you eat healthy all the time. No one will be the wiser that this is the first time you have eaten a leafy green since your wife left you. The crisp, refreshing arugula will certainly overpower the stench that has been accumulated in and around you since your last depressive episode. Bon apetit!

Ingredients

  • Arugula
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Dressing

Steps

  1. Rince leafy greens and place in bowl
  2. Chop the iceberg lettuce so it is bite sized
  3. Add the dressing to the greens in a bowl
  4. Toss and serve
  5. Consume
